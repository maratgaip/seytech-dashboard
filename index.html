CAREER

CAREER SERVICES

Career Services is how we support our graduates to find their first role as a developer. Our team will work with you to identify your unique career goals, and guide you to success.

Career Coaching

We’ll help you cultivate your career goals and layout a plan of attack. Based on your level we come up with the best career plan which suits you. We never give up on you and will work with you till you find a job. We will keep working with you even after you find a job.

Resume prep

You will have endless opportunities to practice your elevator pitch and we’ll show you how to highlight your assets. Career coaching will help you to prepare a resume and improve your chance to be contacted by recruiters.

Interviews

We will do mock interviews and prepare you to all tech companies by improving your communication and technical skills. Career coaching will train you how to answer cultural and technical questions during interviews by providing lots of samples.

Mentorship

Our mentorship doesn’t end when you find a job. We continue to support students during their first month at work. We even support alumni to find better job placement and/or find more senior-level positions in software engineering.
